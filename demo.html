Why Choose

Adult Day Care?

Our facility is a place for seniors to stay active, healthy and happy during the day. Respect, compassion, dignity, kindness, a commitment to safety and providing genuine care is our philosophy at Princeton Adult Day Care. Please visit our Contact page to request more information.

Daily Scheduled Activities Include:

  • Exercise

  • Yoga and Zumba

  • ​Book Club

  • Arts and Crafts

  • Games           

  • Physical Therapy

  • Music

  • Movies

  • Guest Speakers

  • Library Area

  • Birthday and Holiday Celebrations

  • And more...
